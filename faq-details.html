Implementing Cloud-Based Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Enhanced Security

Authored by Dicholo
15/11/2023

To enable MFA, ensure you have a reliable cloud-based authentication service integrated with your application. Popular choices include Amazon Cognito, Azure AD Multi-Factor Authentication, or Google Identity Platform.

  • Access 'Security Settings' in your Cloud Application Dashboard.
  • Under 'Multi-Factor Authentication,' choose the desired cloud-based solution.
  • Follow the provider's instructions to configure and activate MFA for your users.
  • Encourage users to use a secure authenticator app for additional verification.

Note: For any issues with MFA, reach out to our support team for assistance, including guidance on resetting MFA settings.
